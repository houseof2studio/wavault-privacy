Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 11, 2025

Version: 1.0

At WaVault, we adhere to a strict "Zero-Knowledge" architecture. This means we have designed our software so that we technically cannot access your data, even if we are compelled to do so.

1. Introduction and Scope

WaVault ("The Extension") is a browser extension tool designed to organize locally stored data. This Privacy Policy explains what data interacts with the Extension and how that data is secured.

2. The "Local-First" Architecture

Unlike traditional cloud software, WaVault operates entirely within your browser's Local Runtime Environment.

  • No Cloud Uploads: Contact data extracted from WhatsApp Web is processed in your device's memory (RAM) and stored in your browser's Local Storage (chrome.storage.local).
  • No Backend Database: WaVault does not maintain a database of user contacts. We have no "Admin Panel" to view your saved leads.

3. Data Collection

We collect minimal data required for account administration:

  • Account Info: Email address and License Key (for Pro verification).
  • Telemetry: Anonymous usage diagnostics (e.g., "Extension crashed on export") to improve stability. This does not include any user-generated content.

We DO NOT collect: Phone numbers, chat messages, group names, or contact photos.

4. Data Processing Permissions

To function, WaVault requires specific permissions in Chrome:

  • storage: To save your structured data locally.
  • unlimitedStorage: To support databases larger than 5MB.
  • activeTab: To interact with the WhatsApp Web interface only when you initiate the extension.

5. Third-Party Subprocessors

We utilize the following trusted third parties for infrastructure:

  • Chrome Web Store: For secure distribution and automatic updates.
  • Stripe (or Merchant of Record): For PCI-compliant payment processing. We never store credit card numbers.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Since we do not store your data, "Right to Erasure" requests are handled by you simply uninstalling the extension. Once uninstalled, all locally stored data is permanently deleted by the browser.
