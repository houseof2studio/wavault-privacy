1. The Instant Connection

WaVault eliminates the "save-to-phone" friction. Use the Start Chat feature (located in the top icon bar) to message any number globally without adding them to your permanent phone contacts.

Global Entry

Supports international E.164 formats automatically. Just type the number and enter.

Zero-Footprint

Messages are sent through the WhatsApp Web bridge, keeping your mobile address book clean.

2. Precision Scanning

The core of WaVault is its Intelligence Engine. When you hit the scan icon, the extension performs a non-intrusive metadata sweep of your active WhatsApp session.

  • IDLE State: The extension remains dormant to save CPU until a scan is triggered.
  • Security Sandbox: All data is structured locally using the compiled Rust WASM core.
  • Real-time Progress: Visual feedback as the engine identifies Saved, Unsaved, and Business contacts.
Pro Tip The scan doesn't upload your messages. It only reads the contact identifiers and labels you see on screen to build your local workspace.

3. Tab Management

Organize your leads through three specialized, high-performance perspectives. WaVault uses Virtual Scrolling to handle lists of tens of thousands without lag.

Contacts Tab

Your main database. Divided into "My Contacts" and "Others" for clear lead prioritization.

Groups Tab

Aggregates all your active groups by member count. Perfect for niche community management.

Country Tab

An intelligent geographical breakdown. See exactly where your leads are coming from globally with automatic flag recognition.

4. Search & Filter

Locate any specific lead in milliseconds. The WaVault search bar performs a multi-field "Fuzzy Search" across names, phone numbers, and WhatsApp Labels.

  • Deep Search: The engine queries against a normalized _searchStr to ensure no match is missed.
  • Status Filtering: Instantly isolate "Business" or "Unsaved" contacts via the sub-navigation headers.

5. Selection Engine

WaVault implements Scoped Selection (Best Practice). This ensures you only export exactly what you intend to, preventing data pollution.

  • Select All: A smart checkbox that selects only the items currently visible in your filtered view.
  • Avatar Toggle: Click an avatar to enter "Selection Mode." Snap animations provide tactile feedback.
  • Clear-on-Switch: Switching tabs (e.g., from Groups to Countries) clears your current selection to keep your export "clean."

6. The Export Ecosystem

The Unified Export Bar at the bottom of the interface is your gateway to data portability. It provides three distinct formats, each optimized for a specific business workflow.

A11Y Standard The entire export flow is keyboard-accessible (WCAG AA), featuring clear focus states for screen reader compatibility.

7. Format Specialties

Deep analysis of why WaVault offers three distinct file types:

Excel Microsoft Excel (.xlsx)

The Analyst's Choice. Best for CRM imports (Salesforce, HubSpot) and lead analysis.

  • CRM-Ready: Auto-formatted headers and sanitized sheet names.
  • Smart Columns: Optimized column widths and data validation.
  • Data Cleaning: Automatically removes redundant WhatsApp metadata for a clean spreadsheet.

CSV Comma Separated Values (.csv)

The Universal Bridge. Best for developers, data scientists, and legacy systems.

  • RFC Compliant: Guaranteed to work with any coding language or script (Python, R, Node).
  • Lightweight: Zero overhead. Just raw, structured data.

VCard Virtual Contact File (.vcf)

The Mobile Native. Best for direct-to-phone contact syncing.

  • 1-Click Import: Open the file on your iPhone or Android to instantly add hundreds of leads to your phone book.
  • Standardized: Follows global contact sharing standards.
