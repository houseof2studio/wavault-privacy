Lead Utility Architecture

WaVault is engineered to bridge the gap between your browser and your professional CRM. We focus on eliminating friction and safeguarding your leads through local, high-speed lead extraction.

1. Direct Messaging Logic

Scale your outreach without the technical debt of a cluttered contact book. WaVault transforms how you interact with numbers in the wild.

Magic Context Launcher

Access a direct bridge to WhatsApp from any professional website. Stop the cycle of manual data entry.

  • Highlight: Select any phone number on any CRM, portal, or website.
  • Right-Click: Access the "Chat on WhatsApp" instant menu.
  • Connect: Launch a conversation immediately without saving the contact.

The Direct Messenger

Designed for rapid-fire communication when you already have the lead data.

  • Interface: Tap the chat icon in the top-right of your WaVault header.
  • Frictionless: Paste any international number and strike while the lead is hot.

2. Lead Intelligence Dashboard

WaVault doesn't just list data; it builds your intelligence. Turn high-volume chat history into actionable business assets.

Hyper-Sync Discovery

Structure months of conversation data into a clean, searchable dashboard in seconds.

  • Auto-Classification: Automatically distinguish between Business API accounts and private users.
  • Unsaved Discovery: Find all the "external" numbers hidden in your chats and groups that aren't in your official contacts yet.
Dashboard Analytics The Hero Stats at the top of your dashboard track your conversion pipeline. Monitor unsaved leads versus saved customers to measure your growth.

3. Metadata & Identity Extraction

Gain insights before you even start the conversation. WaVault extracts professional markers that are usually invisible.

Push-Name Capture

WaVault retrieves the "Push Name" users have set for themselves. This is a goldmine for identifying the person behind an unsaved phone number.

"About" Status Visibility

Instantly see the "About" status (formerly 'Status') for every lead. Professionals often list their job titles, company names, or websites here.

4. Global Market Targeting

Expand your reach with built-in geographic classification. Perfect for running localized marketing campaigns or sorting by time-zone.

Regional View Navigation

Manage thousands of contacts across different countries with ease.

  • Visual Aids: Instant flag recognition for over 200 countries.
  • Smart Sorting: Group your leads by country code to prioritize your focal regions.

Group Extraction Plus

Extract every participant from your professional networking groups with a single click. Every member becomes a structured lead in your vault.

5. Selection Flow & Search

Built for the power user who handles hundreds of leads daily. Our selection engine is optimized for high-speed, bulk workflows.

High-Speed Controls

  • Avatar Tapping: Click any profile picture to select—no precision hunting for small boxes.
  • Global Search: Search by Name, Phone, or even their personal Metadata instantly.

6. CRM Export Vault

Your data is only valuable if it's portable. Export your refined lead lists into standard professional formats for your CRM.

Unlimited Professional Formats

  • Excel (.xlsx): Formatted with headers and column-autofilters for Salesforce or HubSpot.
  • CSV: The universal format for bulk automation and mass-uploaders.
  • VCard (.vcf): Sync your browser-discovered leads to your mobile device in one tap.

7. Unlimited Zero-Cost Access

We are currently providing WaVault as a 100% Free Utility for all professionals. Every feature listed in this guide is fully accessible without any subscription or hidden fees.

Full Capability Suite

  • No Export Limits: Export as many leads as your business requires.
  • Full Metadata Access: View all names, about statuses, and geographic data without restricted previews.
  • Community Project: Enjoy the full power of a premium enterprise tool as part of our early-access community.

8. Security & Stealth Protocol

We respect your professional privacy. WaVault is designed so that your client data stays exactly where it should: on your machine.

Zen Mode (Stealth)

Share your screen or record demos without leaking sensitive client information.

  • Windows Shortcut: Alt + Shift + P
  • Mac Shortcut: Opt + Shift + P
  • Automatic Masking: All names, phone numbers, and profile images are blurred in real-time.
Privacy Standard 100% of lead extraction happens in your browser's local memory. No contact data ever touches our servers. Your vault is private by design.
