Common Questions

Is my data reliable and safe?

Yes. WaVault is a "Zero-Knowledge" extension. We do not have servers, and we never see your contacts. All processing happens locally on your computer inside the Chrome browser.

Can I export contacts from groups?

Absolutely. The "Bulk Group Export" feature allows you to open any WhatsApp group and download the entire participant list to Excel or CSV in seconds.

Do I need to pay for the basic features?

No. The "Starter" plan is free forever and allows you to backup your saved contacts. The Pro plan is for power users who need to export unsaved numbers and groups.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime directly from the extension settings or by emailing support. You will retain access until the end of your billing period.
